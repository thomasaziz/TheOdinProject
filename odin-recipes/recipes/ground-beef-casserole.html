Ground Beef Casserole

Description

Old-fashioned hamburger casserole is comfort food at its best! Simple recipe featuring ground beef, noodles and a creamy cheese sauce.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Seasonings
  • Pasta
  • Sour cream
  • Cream cheese
  • Onion
  • Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Cook and season the ground beef
  2. Cook egg noodles
  3. Mix sour cream and cream cheese
  4. Assemble and Bake
