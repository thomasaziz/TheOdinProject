Mac and Cheese


Description

Classic baked mac and cheese recipe with a buttered bread crumb topping. Creamy, comforting and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Seasonings
  • Breadcrumbs

Steps

  1. Boil the macaroni in salted water until the noodles are al dente.
  2. Make the cheese sauce by mixing melted butter with flour, whisking in milk and then stirring in the cheese.
  3. Melt two tablespoons of butter in a skillet, add the breadcrumbs and toast until the crumbs are brown. Spread this topping over the mac and cheese.
  4. Bake in a preheated oven until the topping is golden brown.
