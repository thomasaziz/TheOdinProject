Pasta Salad

Description

Pasta salad recipe featuring bell peppers, cherry tomatoes and olives. Great for barbecues!

Ingredients

  • Tri-colored spiral pasta
  • Italian-style salad dressing
  • Salad seasoning mix
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Bell peppers (green, red and yellow)
  • Black olives

Steps

  1. Cook pasta until tender in a pot of lightly salted boiling water
  2. Rinse cooked pasta under cold water and drain
  3. Whisk Italian dressing and salad spice mix together until smooth
  4. Combine pasta, tomatoes, olives and peppers in a bowl
  5. Mix dressing into the salad and toss to coat
  6. Refrigerate salad for at least 8 hours
